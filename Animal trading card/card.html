Red Fox

Foxes have a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed, slightly upturned snout, and a long bushy tail.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Speed: 50Km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 2-5 years
  • Japanese Word: Kitsune

The red fox (Vulpes vulpes) is the largest of the true foxes and one of the most widely distributed members of the order Carnivora.It is listed as least concern by the IUCN.The red fox originated from smaller-sized ancestors from Eurasia during the Middle Villafranchian period.
